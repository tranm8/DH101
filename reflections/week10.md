# Week 10 Reflection

Reflect 10: How does AI change our relationship to play, rules, and narrative?

Right now, it is making changes by adding "dynamic rules." In many games today, everything is already scripted and follows a set path. But AI allows for unpredictable outcomes. For example, if we use AI for dialogue, the player can type anything and the AI will give a different answer every time. This changes play from following a fixed map to a real time conversation. However, AI is not yet at a "human standard" because it still lacks the deep quality of human writing.
