# Week 9 Reflection

Reflect 9: What is creativity when AI can generate endlessly? Where is the human in generative work?

When AI can generate endlessly, the beauty of curation appears. In a world of infinite options, true creativity is having the heart to listen, feel, touch, read and choose the one piece of art that truly matters. The human now is the director who gives the artwork its soul. A machine only understands data and patterns, which easily leads to a boring averageness. It takes human agency to set the rules, give the work real meaning, and recognize a beautiful happy accident when the machine makes a mistake. 

I generated 9 tracks when did make 8, but quickly noticed the AI often creates a boring averageness. I had to listen very patiently to find the tracks with real feeling. Only partially, and that was the best part. I controlled the parameters (the acoustic vibe, the story), but the AI surprised me with the exact notes.
