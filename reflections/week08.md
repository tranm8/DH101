# Week 8 Reflection

Reflect 8: Who is visible/invisible in AI networks? How does visualization reveal or obscure power?

In AI networks, the visiblization are usually big tech companies, investors, and famous engineers. Companies like Google, OpenAI, and Microsoft often appear in maps of AI power. However, many people who make AI possible remain invisible, such as data labelers, content moderators, miners extracting minerals for hardware, and communities affected by data centers or environmental impacts. Visualization can both reveal and obscure power. Maps and graphs can obscure power because they often highlight companies or cities while leaving out hidden labor, supply chains, and environmental costs. As a results, visualizations may make AI appear driven only by innovation and technology, while masking the social and ecological systems that support it.
