# Week 2 Reflection

Reflect 2: Can AI help us define what it means to be human, or does it reduce us to patterns?

AI does not define humans; AI is creating challenges that force us to learn how to redefine ourselves. For example, when using social media or shopping, AI algorithms view us as a collection of habits. If we rely too heavily on AI suggestions, we will unintentionally lose the spontaneity and unpredictability inherent in being human. At that point, we are being defined by AI. But if you always know how to ask 'why' questions, then AI will be a tool that highlights your true humanity.
