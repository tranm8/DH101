# Week 3 Reflection

Reflect 3: What does “authenticity” mean when identity is co-constructed with AI?

AI is too perfect, causing the erosion of real identity; when we co-construct identity with AI, we inadvertently fall into the trap of averageness. AI operates based on probability and crowd data, therefore everything created by AI always tends toward the average. So if you do not have your own opinions, and all your choices are based on the machine's suggestions, then that self is essentially a product of the system, and you are no longer the creator, but merely the approver of algorithms.
