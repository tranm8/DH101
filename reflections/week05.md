# Week 5 Reflection

Reflect 5: How does AI alter authorship and remix culture? Who owns AI-made art?

AI alters authorship because art creation is no longer an individual process. The human provides the concept and guidance, while the AI produces the artwork. Therefore, the artist is more of a director or editor. AI makes remix culture bigger and faster. It can mix styles and ideas in seconds, which makes creativity easier but also more complex. Who owns AI made art is still unclear. Usually, the person who gives meaningful creative input can claim it. But since AI is trained on many existing works, authorship feels shared.
