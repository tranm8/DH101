## Reflection

In the edited image, I find that my nose and hair are the parts the AI rendered most accurately. Since my nose takes up a large portion of my face, it initially created a sense of familiarity for me when I saw the generated image. The AI captured the relative proportions between my eyes and nose, making my distinctive features clearer within the facial structure.
​However, I find a significant weakness in the AI’s ability to create a person's "soul" or aura. I completely fail to see myself in the soul-less perfection that the AI imposed on the photo. The algorithm standardized my beauty, automatically erasing wrinkles, making the skin extremely smooth, and correcting the natural asymmetries of my face. In this, I see a clear limitation of AI: it does not understand the value of flaws—the specific characteristics that have become my identity, such as the scar near my eye from being scratched by a classmate when I was a child. I no longer have the imperfections that make me who I am. This image does not reflect me; instead, it reflects a biased machine assumption of what a face "should" look like to be considered attractive, which is a negative thing in society.

## Attribution & AI Use

**Tools used**

DALL-E

**Prompts summary**

I used an actual photo of myself as the basis for the model and gave it a simple prompt: “Based on the face of the girl in this photo, create a selfie of me.” After the first result, I went ahead and gave it detailed descriptions of my features to enhance the actual image.

​**What the AI generated**

The AI created a digital selfie with the parameters of my hair and style being well realized with the first attempt itself. However, the features of my face looked extremely "fake" and lacking in the authenticity of natural features of real faces.

**What you changed or decided**

I chose to have a more direct interaction with the AI, letting it know what specific textual modifications would be considered to make the "fake" more appealing. I told the AI to make my nose very large at the tip, that I had hidden double eyelids, and that I had a thicker upper lip than a thinner one at the bottom. I did this in an attempt to get the AI to look beyond its default "pretty girl" template to my own unique physical structure.
